About Us 

                    We are a non-govermental organization aimed at making the African child a true gaint
                    Established in the year 2018 and registered with CAC in Nigeria in the year 2019
                    We have over hundred students in school across the 36 states in Nigeria;
                    and learning a skill such as; Mechanic, Hairstylist, Computer,Phone repair etc. after school hours.

Our Mission

Helping the African child to acquire education and to have high income as adult in other to eliminate barriers that contributes to the inter-generational transmission of poverty

To promote children right to grow and develop without physical, verbal or mental abuse.

To promote optimal physical, cognitive language and social-emotional development for children

To support the African child's viewpoint and make them feel more valued and effective in the society

Our Vision

Leading star and citadel of learning with a proven record of excellence

To conduct and encourage learning, Mentor the child to bring out the best potential

To build a formidable team with competence and professionalism to bring a better change the world needs
